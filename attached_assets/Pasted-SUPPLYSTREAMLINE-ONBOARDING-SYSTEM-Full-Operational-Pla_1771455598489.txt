SUPPLYSTREAMLINE ONBOARDING SYSTEM
Full Operational Plan
Purpose (Short Summary)

This app is a structured onboarding engine designed to turn scraped vendor and buyer leads into signed-up SupplyStreamline members through disciplined cold calling, email follow-up, tracking, and measurable conversion stages.

Its purpose is to:

• Organize large volumes of scraped leads
• Control daily calling workflow
• Enforce structured follow-ups
• Track every call and email
• Automatically mark completed signups
• Provide real conversion metrics from call → signup

The system is built to eliminate randomness and create a measurable, repeatable acquisition machine.

Core Concept

Every lead moves through a structured lifecycle:

New → Called → Emailed → Clicked → Signed Up → Completed

Nothing is left untracked.
Nothing disappears.
Every action is measurable.

Roles
Admin

Responsible for:

Uploading and organizing leads

Assigning batches to callers

Monitoring performance

Managing email templates

Reviewing metrics

Managing users

Caller (Vendor or Buyer)

Responsible for:

Calling assigned leads

Logging outcomes

Capturing correct email address

Sending follow-up emails

Working retry queue daily

Each caller has their own login and workspace.

Lead Intake & Organization
Upload

Admin uploads XLSX files scraped from Google Maps.

During upload:

Fields are mapped (company name, phone, website, place_id, email, rating, etc.)

Leads are deduplicated

Each lead is assigned a unique tracking token

Leads are categorized into:

Vendor pipeline

Buyer pipeline

Each pipeline is handled independently.

Assignment System

Admin assigns daily batches to callers.

Assignment options:

Filter by state

Filter by category

Filter by rating

Filter by has phone

Filter by has email

Sort by priority

Select number of leads

Callers can also self-pull leads if admin has not prepared a batch.

No caller is ever blocked from working.

Caller Daily Workflow

Callers work from a structured “Today View” dashboard.

Tabs:

NEW

Leads never called.

RETRY

Leads previously attempted and scheduled for retry.
Includes:

Eligible today

Scheduled for future retry

ACTIVE / PENDING

Leads that were spoken to and are in progress:

Send Info

Follow Up

Interested

COMPLETED

Leads that have successfully signed up.

Unreachable leads are hidden by default and visible via toggle.

Lead Detail Page (Central Workspace)

When a caller opens a lead, they see:

Company information

Phone number (editable)

Website

Category

Rating & reviews

Hours

Call history

Notes

Email history

Signup status

This is the control center for that lead.

Call Process
Step 1 — Open Lead

Caller reviews company info and prepares.

(If AI research is available, caller can generate a tailored opener script.)

Step 2 — Click “Call”

Inside the lead page is a Call button.

When clicked:

A call modal opens

Phone number is shown and editable

Opener script is visible (if generated)

Last call attempt is displayed

Attempt count is visible

Caller places the call.

Step 3 — Log Outcome

After call, caller selects outcome from fixed list:

Retry outcomes:

No Answer

Voicemail

Gatekeeper

Call Dropped

Positive outcomes:

Spoke – Send Info

Spoke – Follow Up

Spoke – Interested

Final negative:

Spoke – Not Interested

Caller adds notes if necessary.

Automatic Logic

If retry outcome:

Attempt count increases

Retry scheduled in 2 business days

Max 3 attempts

After max attempts → marked Unreachable

If “Not Interested”:

Marked unreachable immediately

If positive outcome:

Lead moves to ACTIVE / PENDING

All logic is automatic. Caller does not manually manage retries.

Email Process

Inside the Lead Detail page is an Email tab.

Email buttons appear based on eligibility:

Send Info

Requires:

At least one call logged

Confirmed email captured

Not unreachable

Follow Up

Requires:

Send Info already sent

Confirmed email present

Unreachable Outreach

Requires:

Marked unreachable or max attempts reached

Any email available

Emails are sent from:
connect@supplystreamline.com

Templates are controlled by Admin and can include variables such as:

Company name

Caller name

Signup link

Each email is logged and tracked.

Email Tracking

System tracks:

Sent

Opened

Clicked

Bounced

Status updates automatically.

Each lead has a unique tracking link embedded in the email.

Signup Completion

When a lead signs up through the tracked link and confirms email:

The system receives a webhook

Lead is marked SIGNED_UP

signed_up_at timestamp is stored

Lead moves to COMPLETED tab

This closes the lifecycle.

Metrics & Accountability

The system measures:

For Callers:

Calls placed per day

Emails sent per day

Call → Email conversion %

For Admin:

Emails opened %

Click %

Click → Signup %

Signups today / week / month

Signups per caller

This transforms calling into measurable performance.

Timing Intelligence

System warns if call is placed within:

15 minutes of opening

15 minutes of closing

Call timing is logged for future analysis.

No duplicate leads

Clean movement from stage to stage

Every lead lives in exactly one structured state.
