NO_ANSWER

VOICEMAIL

GATEKEEPER

CALL_DROPPED

If outcome is:

SPOKE_NOT_INTERESTED
→ lead.unreachable = true

If outcome is:

SPOKE_SEND_INFO or SPOKE_FOLLOW_UP or SPOKE_INTERESTED
→ do NOT mark unreachable.
