Full Email Handling System — Detailed Operational Plan
🎯 Purpose of Email System

The email system exists to:

• Convert calls into signups
• Keep all communication centralized
• Track every engagement event
• Provide measurable conversion metrics
• Prevent callers from using outside email tools

Everything must live inside this app.

1️⃣ Core Email Philosophy

All outbound emails are sent from:

connect@supplystreamline.com


All inbound replies must:

Appear inside the app

Be visible on the lead page

Be part of that lead’s email thread

No Gmail.

No external inbox.

No fragmented communication.

2.2 Gating Rules
SEND_INFO

Allowed only if:

At least one call logged

Confirmed email exists

Lead not unreachable

FOLLOW_UP

Allowed only if:

SEND_INFO already sent

Confirmed email exists

UNREACHABLE_OUTREACH

Allowed if:

Unreachable OR max attempts reached

Email exists (confirmed preferred, else scraped)

3️⃣ Inbound Email Handling (What We Discussed)

This is the part that needs to be fully structured.

🎯 Goal

If a lead replies to:

connect@supplystreamline.com


That reply must:

• Be captured automatically
• Be attached to the correct lead
• Appear in the lead’s Email tab
• Appear in a global Email Inbox inside the app
• Trigger activity tracking

4️⃣ Inbound Email Architecture

We discussed using SendGrid for everything.

So inbound email must use:

SendGrid Inbound Parse Webhook

Flow:

Lead replies to email

SendGrid receives reply

SendGrid POSTs to:

/api/sendgrid/inbound


App processes payload

App attaches message to correct lead

5️⃣ Lead Matching Logic

How do we attach reply to correct lead?

We must include hidden identifiers in outbound emails.

Options:

Preferred: Custom Reply-To Address

Outbound email sets:

Reply-To: lead_<lead_token>@reply.supplystreamline.com


Inbound parse receives:

to = lead_<lead_token>@reply.supplystreamline.com

System extracts:

lead_token

Finds lead

Stores email

This is the cleanest method.

Alternative (less ideal)

Parse subject line with embedded token.
Not recommended.

1️⃣1️⃣ Edge Cases

Must handle:

Bounce → mark lead email invalid

Spam report → suppress future emails

Multiple replies → thread all

Forwarded emails → attempt to attach to correct thread

Attachments (store metadata, optional V1 ignore actual file)
