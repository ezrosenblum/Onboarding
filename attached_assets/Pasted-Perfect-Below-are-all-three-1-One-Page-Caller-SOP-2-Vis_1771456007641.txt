# 1️⃣ ONE-PAGE CALLER SOP

*(Give this to your Filipino callers)*

---

## 🎯 Your Mission

Turn assigned companies into signed-up SupplyStreamline members.

You do this by:

1. Calling
2. Getting the correct email
3. Sending signup info
4. Following up properly

Everything you do must be logged inside the system.

---

## 📅 Your Daily Routine

### Step 1 — Open “Today View”

Work in this order:

1. **NEW** (Priority – 80%)
2. **RETRY** (20%)
3. **ACTIVE/PENDING**

---

## ☎️ How to Work a Lead

### Step A — Open the Lead

Review:

* Company name
* Website
* Notes
* Previous calls
* Email status

(If AI script is available, read it.)

---

### Step B — Call

Click **Call** button.

After call:

* Select correct outcome
* Add notes
* Save

---

## 🎯 Call Outcomes

### Retry outcomes (system will retry automatically)

* No Answer
* Voicemail
* Gatekeeper
* Call Dropped

System will:

* Retry in 2 business days
* Max 3 attempts

---

### Positive outcomes

* Spoke – Send Info
* Spoke – Follow Up
* Spoke – Interested

If positive:

* Get best email address
* Enter in Confirmed Email field
* Click **Send Info**

---

### Final Negative

* Spoke – Not Interested

Lead becomes unreachable.

---

## 📧 Sending Email

Go to **Email tab**.

Buttons will activate when allowed.

### Send Info

Only after:

* You spoke with someone
* You have correct email

### Follow Up

Only after:

* Send Info was already sent

---

## 🧠 Rules

* Never send email without calling first (unless unreachable outreach).
* Always log call immediately.
* Always enter best email.
* Always add notes if useful.
* Work retries daily.
* If admin didn’t assign leads, pull from list.

---

## 🏁 When Is a Lead Done?

When:

* They sign up
* OR they are unreachable
* OR clearly not interested

System will move them automatically.
